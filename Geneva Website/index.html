Geneva

In this brochure, you can find out all about Geneva, and what you can find there.

Places to visit

There are many beautiful lakes near geneva, many of which often are home to boats and marinas, and are used for fishing. Some locations that are set for tourists are the Jet d'Eau, the L'horloge fleurie, and a museum for contemporary art, MAMCO.

Miscellaneous Information

Geneva is the capital of Switzerland, and is home to many beautiful lakes, such as the one pictured on the left. What you many not know, however, is that Switzerland is also known for may other things, like its chocolate, cheese, and banks.

Geneva is also home to castles, of which the architecture has been perfected through generations of improving their craft.

The city holds a festival nearly every day celebrating something new. A few examples of this are the celebration fo the defeat of Napoleon, and the celebration of Geneva's entrance into the Old Swiss Confederacy.

In Geneva, most travelers get access to a Geneva Transport Card, which allows for tourists to get public transport for free, as well as visit tourist locations for a discounted rate.
